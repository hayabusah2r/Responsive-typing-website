Features

✺ Distraction-free focus

The interface is made as simple and possible. Dark background is applied for decreasing contrast. We have eliminated distractions so that you can concentrate, be more productive, and get your writing done

✺ Write as much as you like

Typist lets you save an unlimited number of documents of any length. No word and character limits. All of your writing is saved online so that it's available no matter where you are or what computer you use. You never have to worry about running out of storage space.

✺ All writing is private and secure

All of your writing is transmitted securely to our servers and backed up nightly. The website ensures your privacy. No one except you will be able to access the document.

✺ Save text option

You can save/export your writing as .txt file. Then you can convert it to pdf or docx or anything you want.

✺ No ads

This website is completely ad-free. So, you can give your full concentration to work.
